##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Tikka:

1. Take 1/2 kg Yogurt
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Vinegar
1. Prepare  Oil As Required
1. Make ready 1 Packet National Chicken Tikka Recipe Mix
